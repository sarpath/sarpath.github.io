Maa Durga ka sada rahe ashirvad,dhan,
samridhi,sukh aur kamiyabi ka de aapko aashirvad
Navrati ki nau raatein roshan kar dein aapka Jeevan !




Maa Durga ke ashirwaad se apka jivan sukhmay ho
Is Navratri pe hamari shubkamnayen apke saath hai.











Jagat Paalan Haar Hai Maa,
Mukti Ka Dham Hai Maa.
Hamari Bakti Ke Adhar Hai Maa
Ham Sab Ki Raksha Ki Avatar Hai Maa.






Humko Tha Intazar Wo Ghadi Aa Gai,
Hokar Singh Par Sawar Mata Rani Aa Gai,
Hogi Ab Mann Ki Har Muraad Puri
Harne Ab Saare Dukh Mata Dware Aa Gai.
